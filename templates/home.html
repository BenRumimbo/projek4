Welcome to Litera Lab, an educational platform dedicated to assisting schools and students in preparing for the new challenges in the national education evaluation system.


With the removal of the National Examination and its replacement with the Assessmen Kompetensi Minimum (AKM), schools are tasked with the important responsibility of preparing students to face these changes. One of the primary emphases of the Assessmen Nasional (AN) is to assess the level of literacy skills among students, a crucial aspect in education. Litera Lab is designed to address this need by providing schools and students with resources, exercises aimed at enhancing their literacy skills. With a variety of resources available, we are committed to supporting an effective and enjoyable learning process for all parties involved. Explore Litera Lab, where literacy lays the foundation for students' future success!

Our Frame Work

Our Frame Work

Litera Lab was developed using the AKM framework, which comprises Content, Context, and Cognitive Processes.

Content

Litera Lab provides reading materials in the form of both fiction and informative texts

Context

Litera Lab provides reading materials within the contexts of Personal, Socio-cultural, and Scientific aspects.

Cognitif Proceses

Cognitive Processes: Litera Lab prepares questions at three cognitive levels: Finding Information, Interpreting/Integrating, and Evaluating and Reflecting.
